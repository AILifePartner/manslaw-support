Man's Law

The Unwritten Rule

About the App

Man's Law is a hilarious 2-player pass-and-play party game based on the unwritten rule every man follows — never stand next to someone at the urinals.

Take turns picking your spot, block your opponent, and prove who truly understands Man's Law.

How to Play

  • A row of urinals appears on screen
  • Players take turns choosing where to stand
  • You cannot stand next to an occupied urinal (Man's Law!)
  • If you can't place on your turn, you lose
  • Choose Bo1, Bo3, or Bo5 format

FAQ

Q: Is it free?

A: Yes! Man's Law is completely free with ads.

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All data (player stats, match history) is stored locally on your device only. No server, no account needed.

Q: Can I play with more than 2 players?

A: The game is designed for 2 players, but you can take turns with friends in a tournament style!
